Intranet

An intranet is a private network used within an organization to share information, resources, and services securely among employees.

It uses the same technologies as the internet (such as web browsers and HTTP), but it is restricted to authorized users only within the organization.

Intranets are commonly used in companies, schools, and government institutions for communication, document sharing, and internal applications.
